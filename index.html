Trapezoid Area Calculator

Use this calculator to find the area of ANY regular trapezoid! Simply out in the values that the calculator is asking for and let it do its thing!

IMPORTANT: Only insert NUMERICAL values (1,2,3) and not words (one, two three).
Also, the calculator will only work positive integers. NO NEGATIVE NUMBERS OR DECIMALS.

To find the area of a trapezoid, the formula is: A = (a+b)*h/2.

a and b are the lengths of the base and top of the trapezoid. h is the height.

Insert the proper values and then click “Calculate” for your answer.
